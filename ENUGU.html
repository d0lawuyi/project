About Enugu

Enugu is the capital of Enugu State in Nigeria. It is located in southeastern Nigeria. Enugu is known for its coal mines and is often referred to as the "Coal City". It has a rich history and is a vibrant city with a lot to offer to visitors.

Things to Do in Enugu

  • Visit the Ngwo Pine Forest
  • Explore the Awhum Waterfall
  • Take a tour of the Udi Hills
  • Experience the nightlife at Independence Layout

Quick Facts

  • Enugu was the capital of the former Eastern Region of Nigeria.
  • The city has a diverse population and is known for its friendly people.
  • Enugu is a major center for trade, commerce, and education in southeastern Nigeria.

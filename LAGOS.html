LAGOS

FACTS ABOUT LAGOS

Lagos is a vibrant and populous city located in Nigeria, West Africa. Here are some facts about Lagos: Population: Lagos is one of the most populous cities in Africa, with an estimated population of over 20 million people as of the latest data. It's not only the largest city in Nigeria but also the seventh fastest-growing city in the world..

LAGOS Port City

It's a significant port city, with the Port of Lagos being one of the busiest and largest ports in Africa. The port plays a crucial role in Nigeria's economy, handling a substantial portion of the country's imports and exports.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!

Activities to do in BENIN

Benin City National Museum

Explore the museums extensive collection of artifacts, including bronze sculptures, terracotta artworks, and historical relics that showcase the regions rich cultural heritage

Attend traditional festivals

Experience the vibrant culture of Benin City by participating in traditional festivals such as the Igue Festival, which celebrates the coronation of the Oba and features colorful processions, dances, and rituals.

Visit the National Institute for Cultural Orientation

Learn more about Nigerian culture and heritage by visiting NICO, which offers exhibitions, workshops, and educational programs promoting cultural awareness and appreciation.
